Selected Project Experience

Inactive Landfill Superfund Site, Pennsylvania

Develops figures for deliverables visualizing and presenting data collected during a long-term groundwater monitoring (O&M) program for an inactive landfill and Superfund Site. Graphics created to assist in the management of the project include groundwater potentiometric surface maps, chemical isocontour maps, and topographic and waste thickness isopleth maps. Assists in bi-annual groundwater sampling program by conducting low-flow sampling of monitoring wells, gauging groundwater elevations, and performing landfill cap, gas vent system, and recovery well system inspections.
